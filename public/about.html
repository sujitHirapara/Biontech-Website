Our Values

Purification Method

Biontech Water Ionizers use a process called electrolysis to separate water into alkaline and acidic streams. While this can improve the pH of water, it doesn't remove any minerals from the water.

Health Benefit

Biontech Water Ionizers often maintain or even increase the mineral content in the alkaline water they produce, which may provide potential health benefits.

pH Level Adjustment

Biontech Water Ionizers can increase the pH of water, making it more alkaline. Some people believe that drinking alkaline water may have health benefits, although scientific evidence is limited.
