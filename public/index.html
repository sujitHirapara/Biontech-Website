Benefits of Biontech Ionized Alkaline Water

Anti-oxidant Molecular H2

Biontech ionized hydrogen-rich alkaline water features a selective antioxidant known as Molecular Hydrogen. This Molecular Hydrogen is abundant in electrons, offering a negative oxidation-reduction potential (-ORP).

Natural Minerals

Biontech ionized hydrogen-rich alkaline water is abundant in essential alkaline minerals like calcium, magnesium, potassium, and sodium.

Micro-clustered Water

Biontech ionized hydrogen-rich alkaline water is micro-clustered water. Thanks to its micro-clustered properties, it can penetrate cells and major organs in the human body faster than regular tap or RO water.

Alkaline Water
